Traditional coffee

At Gursha Restaurant, we proudly uphold Ethiopia's rich cultural heritage by offering an authentic Ethiopian coffee ceremony. Our traditional coffee ceremony is a time-honored ritual that invites you to experience the true essence of Ethiopian hospitality. Immerse yourself in the aromatic allure of freshly roasted coffee beans as they're skillfully prepared and brewed in the traditional clay pot, the "jebena." Delight in the captivating process of grinding the beans by hand, creating a rich and robust coffee blend known as "bunna." Under the guidance of our expert hosts, indulge in this ceremonial affair, where each step represents a cherished tradition passed down through generations. As the aroma of incense fills the air, the ceremony unfolds, fostering an atmosphere of warmth, connection, and shared stories. Join us at Gursha Restaurant to partake in this timeless Ethiopian tradition, where every sip of our meticulously brewed coffee tells a story of cultural heritage and camaraderie. Experience the authenticity and warmth of Ethiopia's coffee culture right here in our establishment.
